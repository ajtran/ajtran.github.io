Prokudin-Gorskii Images:

Given red, green, and blue layers of a colored image, I found the best alignment of the three channels and recreated the colored image. In order to find the shifts that resulted in the best image, I used a pyramid algorithm and calculated the smallest sum of squared differences at each level of the pyramid. I optimized and refined the search by removing white spaces along the borders of each color channel to focus the alignment on the center of the image.
