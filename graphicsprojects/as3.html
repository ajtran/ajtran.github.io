arch.bez.txt

./as3 arch.bez.txt .1

The images from left to right display uniform subdivison with flat shading (default), (2x) uniform subdivision with smooth shading (press s key), and uniform subdivision with a wire mesh (press w key).

./as3 arch.bez.txt .01 -a

The images from left to right display adaptive tessalation with flat shading (default), adaptive subdivision with smooth shading (press s key), and adaptive subdivision with a wire mesh (press w key).

./as3 teacup.bez.txt .01 -a
